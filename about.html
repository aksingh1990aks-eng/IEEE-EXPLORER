Student Branch Chapter GGV

We are the Microwave Theory and Technology Society at Guru Ghasidas Vishwavidyalaya. Our mission is to advance the understanding of radio frequency, microwave engineering, and antenna design.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between abstract electromagnetic theory and practical, real-world engineering. We build interactive educational platforms like RF Explorer to make invisible frequencies visible and intuitive for the next generation of electronics engineers.

Our Domain

We focus strictly on high-frequency electronics. From fundamental waveguide theory and analog electronics to advanced HFSS simulations of Vivaldi and patch antennas, we push the boundaries of what students can build and analyze in wireless communication.
