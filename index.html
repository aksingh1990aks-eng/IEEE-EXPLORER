About this initiative

Making the
invisible visible
for the next generation.

RF components are the backbone of every modern communication system — 5G networks, satellite navigation, radar, medical imaging. Yet they stay completely hidden from the people who use them most.

RF Explorer was built to change that. Through real-world context, relatable analogies, real fabricated specimens and 3D electromagnetic simulations, we turn abstract physics into something you can actually see, feel and understand.
