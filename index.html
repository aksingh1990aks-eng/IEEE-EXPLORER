About This Platform

Welcome to IEEE MTT-S
RF Learning Hub

The IEEE Microwave Theory and Techniques Society Student Branch Chapter presents a dedicated e-learning platform designed especially for Class 11 and 12 students curious about Radio Frequency and Microwave Engineering.

This platform bridges the gap between school-level physics and professional RF engineering — no prior background required. Every concept is explained from first principles.

From EM waves to designing real-world RF components like Branch Line Couplers, Phase Shifters, and Patch Antennas — structured lectures, visualizations, and simulation guides for high school learners.
